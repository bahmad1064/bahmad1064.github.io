Message from the Dean



Dr. Abdul Azeez AL-Sayyari

The Faculty of Engineering offers a Bachelor of Science degree in three disciplines: electrical, mechanical and civil. The curriculum is designed to support advanced concepts in all fields of engineering in depth and expansion to provide students with the required knowledge in their field of specialization

To achieve these goals, advanced and advanced technical tools are used. The Bachelor's degree is 5 years (including a preparatory year) and includes 132 hours. Undergraduate advisors develop bachelor's and academic records and course requirements for successful student guidance so that they can graduate on time. During classroom breaks,

students are encouraged to participate in an industrial training program in many places in Saudi Arabia so that they can gain experience with practical engineering. The career opportunities for graduates of the Faculty of Engineering are varied and the demand for them is increasing and includes: civil engineer, electrical engineer and mechanical engineer in many departments in the government sector and the private sector as the market is open to new graduates.
